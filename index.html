Frequently Asked Questions

What services does the government portal offer?
Our portal handles a wide range of services including permit applications, business registration, document certification, public records requests, civic support inquiries, and international legalisation. If you are unsure which service you need, contact our office and we will guide you through the correct process.
What documents are required to submit a service request?
Please bring your original identification (national ID, passport, or driver's license) along with any relevant supporting documents for the service you are requesting. Specific requirements vary by service type — confirm exact requirements when submitting your request online or over the phone.
How long does the application process take?
Most standard requests are processed within 3 to 5 business days once all required documents have been verified. Complex applications involving multiple departments or international legalisation may take longer. We maintain full transparency throughout the process and will keep you informed at each stage.
Can I submit and track my application online?
Yes — our digital services portal allows you to submit applications, upload supporting documents, and track your application status in real time. Citizens who are unable to visit our office in person can complete most services entirely online. Contact our helpdesk if you need guidance navigating the portal.
Are there any fees for government services?
Service fees vary depending on the type of application, document complexity, and any additional processing required. We maintain a transparent, published fee schedule accessible on our website and at our office counters. All applicable fees will be clearly communicated before your application is processed.
